## 1. Visual Identity & UI Standards (Minimalist Medical Professional)
* **Color Palette:**
    * **Background:** Clean White / Very Light Gray (#F8F9FA).
    * **Primary Action Color:** Teal (#008080) - used for buttons, active states, and primary icons.
    * **Secondary Color:** Dark Slate (#2F4F4F) - for typography and professional headers.
* **Components:**
    * **Cards:** Soft Rounded Corners (10px radius) with subtle professional shadows.
    * **Typography:** Clean Sans-serif (Roboto or Inter) optimized for both Arabic and English.
    * **Icons:** Modern Filled style for clarity and medical professional feel.
* **Layout:** Fully Responsive Web Design with **Strict RTL (Arabic) Support** while remaining "Multi-language Ready".

---

## 2. Scope of Work (21 Pages Total)
Generate high-fidelity UI views with a **Dynamic Sidebar** that toggles based on User Role:

### A. Public & Authentication (4 Pages)
* **Landing Page:** Professional Hero section, "Book Now" CTA, and Clinic Features.
* **Login:** Secure interface with a 2FA verification step UI.
* **Register:** Step-by-step patient sign-up form.
* **Services Grid:** Detailed dental treatments with pricing badges.

### B. Patient Portal (4 Pages)
* **Dashboard:** Welcome card, "Next Appointment" widget, and health alerts.
* **Booking Wizard:** Single-page tabbed interface (Fast-load) for appointment scheduling.
* **My History:** Comprehensive table view of past visits and digital invoices.
* **Profile:** Secure personal info and medical history view.

### C. Doctor Portal (4 Pages)
* **Medical Dashboard:** Patient volume and productivity charts (**Chart.js** containers).
* **Daily Schedule:** Focused queue of today’s appointments with status indicators.
* **Patient Clinical Hub:** Central workspace for diagnosing and note-taking.
* **Visual Dental Chart:** High-fidelity interactive **SVG Map of 32 teeth**.

### D. Receptionist Portal (4 Pages)
* **Reception Dashboard:** Real-time clinic flow and room occupancy overview.
* **Patient Management:** Search and register tools for walk-in patients.
* **Quick Booking:** Manual scheduling override tool for the front desk.
* **Billing/Invoicing:** Automated invoice generation and payment status UI.

### E. Admin Management (5 Pages)
* **Global Analytics:** Revenue trends and doctor performance charts (**Chart.js**).
* **Staff Management:** CRUD for all accounts, roles, and 2FA settings.
* **Inventory Control:** Stock levels with low-stock visual alerts.
* **Financial Reports:** Debt tracking and monthly revenue statements.
* **Audit Logs:** Detailed system activity tracking table for security.

## 3. Technical Constraints & Instructions
* **Responsiveness:** Must be 100% optimized for Desktop, Tablet, and Mobile.
* **Framework:** Use Clean HTML5 / Bootstrap 5 / Vanilla JS.
* **Goal:** Generate **UI ONLY**. No backend or database connections required at this stage.
* **Localization:** Ensure the layout remains structurally sound for **RTL (Arabic)**.
